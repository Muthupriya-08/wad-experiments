KIT-Kalaignarkarunanidhi Institute of Technology

KalaignarKarunanidhi Institute of Technology (KIT) Coimbatore was established in 2008. The College offers UG and PG Courses such as B.E, M.Tech, MCA and MBA. Apart from regular MCA program, the institute also offers Lateral Entry in MCA. Admission to B.E programs is usually through TNEA and M.Tech selection process will be as per TANCET score followed by TANCA Counselling. Further, admission to MBA and MCA will also be granted as per TANCET score. Application form can be filled in online mode.
